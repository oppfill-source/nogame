Best Odds Engine

Stop settling for whatever line is easiest to find.

The difference between +145 and +155 on the same bet is real money over time. NoGame's best-odds algorithm compares every book's price and marks the winner in green — instantly.

  • Positive odds: higher number is always better
  • Negative odds: closest to zero wins
  • Mixed: +100 beats -105, every time
  • Spreads and totals compared at the same line only

How It Works

From game to best odds
in three steps.

01
Pick a sport and find your game
Browse today's schedule filtered by sport. Live games show the current score and period. Upcoming games show the start time. Use the date selector to look ahead or back.
02
Click the game to see all odds
The game detail page shows every sportsbook's moneyline, spread, and total in a single table. The best available price per outcome is highlighted in green with a ★ Best badge.
03
Click through to the sportsbook
Tap the sportsbook name to open it directly. You already know which book has your number — no more opening five apps to check.
