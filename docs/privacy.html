Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (such as email address during account creation), and information generated automatically when you use the app (such as bet records you log and app usage data).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the NoGame service
  • To send push notifications for high-value AI picks (if you opt in)
  • To calculate and display your betting performance statistics
  • To respond to support requests

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with analytics providers. We use Supabase for database and authentication services, subject to their privacy policy.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS). All connections are encrypted via TLS. Row-level security ensures you can only access your own private data.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing hello@nogame.dev.
